# Getting Started with Piece of Cake 🍰

## Quick Start

1. **Open the application** in your browser
2. You'll see the login/signup page with the chibi cake logo
3. Choose one of these options:

### Option A: Login with Existing User
- Switch to the **"Log In"** tab
- Use any of these test accounts:
  - Username: `baker_bella`
  - Password: `Password123!`
- Click **"Log In"**

### Option B: Create New Account
- Switch to the **"Sign Up"** tab
- Enter a username (3-20 chars, letters/numbers/underscores)
- Enter your email
- Create a password (min 8 chars, must have uppercase, lowercase, number, symbol)
- Click **"Sign Up"**

## First Time User Experience

After logging in, you'll be redirected to the **Feed** page where you can:

### 1. View Slices (Posts)
- See the "For You" feed with 15 slices from 5 different users
- Each slice shows:
  - User's profile picture (avatar with their initial)
  - Username
  - Timestamp (full date and time)
  - Slice content (up to 280 characters)
  - Like, reply, and repost counts
  - Action buttons (heart, comment, repost)

### 2. Navigate the App
The navigation bar at the top has three key elements:
- **Left**: Cake logo + "piece of cake" text → Click to return to feed
- **Center**: Your profile picture → Click to view your profile
- **Right**: "Post Slice" button → Click to create a new slice

**Pro Tip**: The nav bar hides when you scroll down and reappears when you scroll up!

### 3. Create Your First Slice
1. Click the **"Post Slice"** button (top right)
2. A modal will appear with a text area
3. Type your message (1-280 characters)
4. Watch the character counter at the bottom (0/280)
5. Click **"Post Slice"** when ready
6. Your slice appears at the top of the feed!

### 4. Interact with Slices
Click the action buttons on any slice:
- **Heart** (pink/red): Like/Unlike the slice
- **Comment** (blue): View replies and add your own
- **Repost** (green): Repost/Unrepost to share with followers

### 5. Explore Profiles
Click on any username or profile picture to view their profile:
- See their bio and stats (followers/following)
- View all their slices
- Follow/Unfollow them
- Block/Unblock them (if needed)

### 6. View & Reply to Slices
Click anywhere on a slice card to see:
- The full slice
- All replies (oldest first)
- Option to add your own reply

Try clicking on the "pineapple on pizza" debate (by foodie_finn) to see an active conversation!

## Key Features to Explore

### Feed Tabs
At the top of the feed, you'll see two tabs:
- **For You**: Global feed with slices from everyone (20 most recent)
- **Following**: Slices from people you follow + their reposts (20 items)

### Refresh Options
- **Pull to Refresh** (mobile): Pull down at the top of the page
- **Refresh Button**: Scroll to the bottom and click the refresh button

### Your Profile
Click your profile picture (center of nav bar) to:
- View your profile and all your slices
- See your follower/following counts
- Logout (with confirmation)

### Visit Other Profiles
Click any username to visit their profile and:
- Read their bio
- See their slice history
- Follow/Unfollow them
- Block/Unblock them

### Reply Conversations
1. Click on a slice to view it in detail
2. Scroll down to see all replies
3. Click the reply button (blue comment icon) to add your thoughts
4. Your reply appears at the bottom (oldest first)
5. You can like replies too!
6. Delete your own replies with the trash icon

## Tips & Tricks

### Character Counter
- Turns **orange** when you have less than 20 characters left
- Turns **red** when you exceed 280 characters
- You can't post if it's empty or over the limit

### Navigation Shortcuts
- Click the cake logo anytime to return to the feed
- Click your profile picture to quickly access your profile
- Use the back button in your browser to navigate back

### Session Management
- Your session persists even if you close the browser
- After 30 minutes of inactivity, you'll be automatically logged out
- You'll see an alert before being redirected to login

### Follow & Block
- You can't follow yourself (button won't appear on your profile)
- Blocking someone automatically unfollows both ways
- Blocked users won't see your content and vice versa

### Password Reset
If you forget your password:
1. Click "Forgot password?" on the login page
2. Enter your email
3. Check your email for the reset link (in production)
4. Follow the link and enter your new password

## Common Tasks

### How to Post a Slice
1. Click "Post Slice" (top right)
2. Type your message
3. Check the character count
4. Click "Post Slice" button in modal

### How to Reply to a Slice
1. Click on the slice to view details
2. Click the blue comment icon
3. Type your reply
4. Click "Reply" button

### How to Follow Someone
1. Click their username
2. Click "Follow" button on their profile
3. Their slices will appear in your "Following" feed

### How to Block Someone
1. Go to their profile
2. Click "Block" button
3. Confirm the action
4. You'll both be unfollowed automatically

### How to Logout
1. Click your profile picture (nav bar center)
2. Click "Logout" button
3. Confirm in the dialog
4. You'll be redirected to login

## Troubleshooting

### Can't Post a Slice?
- Check that you've written between 1-280 characters
- Look at the character counter - it should not be red
- Make sure you're not posting an empty message

### Session Expired?
- You've been inactive for 30 minutes
- Just log back in to continue

### Can't See New Slices?
- Pull down to refresh (mobile)
- Click the refresh button at the bottom of the feed
- Make sure you're on the correct tab (For You vs Following)

### Navigation Bar Disappeared?
- Scroll up a bit and it will reappear
- Or refresh the page to reset
